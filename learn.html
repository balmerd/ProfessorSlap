Children's Hand Drum Class in Fairfax

Thursday after school 3:30pm - 4:30pm at the Fairfax Community Church

Have fun and learn how to play the hand drum. Using traditional drums of West Africa, Ben will take you on a rhythmic journey.

  • Learn how drum rhythms come together to create music
  • Play games to develop rhythm and explore the excitement of music
  • Learn the history of the drum and the culture where it comes from
But most of all, we will have a great time learning how to make drum music together!
